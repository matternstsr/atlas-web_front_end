Accessible Skip Links: Improving Navigation for Everyone

Published on January 15, 2024 by John Doe

Skip links are an essential part of creating accessible websites. They allow users to navigate past repetitive content and directly access the main content area or other important sections of a page. By including skip links, you can significantly improve the user experience for individuals who rely on assistive technologies such as screen readers or keyboard navigation.
